Feature Interaction of IoT System and Its Security Concerns

15-300 Project Proposal

  • The behavior of an IoT system can be described in terms of largely independent increments called features. A feature may have its own purpose, triggering situation, or intended beneficiary. Many of the threats within the IoT system are manifested at the interaction level, where undesired or malicious coordination between apps and physical devices lead to intricate safety and security issues.
  • My research proposal aims at analyzing the interactions between features, in other words, interactions between applications in the IoT system, and hence detect and address potential threats within such interactions. My approach to the research project is to create a program that utilizes path-sensitive static analysis as well as model checking to automatically generate inter-procedural control flow graphs for every application within the IoT system, and then by checking all graphs as a whole detect if there is dangerous chain of feature interactions.
